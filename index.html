Design

Sophisticated Sound, Elevated Design.

AuraSound Max are crafted from premium materials, blending sleek elegance with a customized fit. From the canopy to the ear cushions, every detail is designed to deliver a luxurious, effortless listening experience.

  • The canopy spanning the headband is made from a breathable knit mesh, distributing weight to reduce on‑head pressure.

  • The stainless steel frame is wrapped with a soft-to-the-touch material for a remarkable combination of strength, flexibility, and comfort.

  • Telescoping arms smoothly extend and stay where you set them, for a consistent fit and seal.

With powerful drivers, adaptive EQ, and spatial audio, AuraSound Max delivers crisp highs, rich bass, and a truly cinematic experience, making every sound come alive with stunning clarity.

Equipped with advanced sensors, AuraSound Max detects when they’re on your head, automatically pausing audio when removed and resuming when you’re ready, for an uninterrupted, intuitive listening experience.

AuraSound Max features precision microphones that filter out background noise, capturing your voice with clarity—even in windy or noisy environments—so every call and command is heard perfectly.

Controls
